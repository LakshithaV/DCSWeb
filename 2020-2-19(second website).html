WELCOME TO DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Jaffna is one of the remarkable centres of teaching and research in Computer Science in Sri Lanka celebrating its 26 Years of Computing. There are currently over 300 undergraduate and few postgraduate students studying within the Department. The Department aims to collaborate in its activities with the IT industrial sector across the country in research and teaching.
